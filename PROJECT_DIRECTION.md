## Who is Aiko

Aiko is a yong (22yo) japanes woman who have live harsh things in her life. She is lost and hopeless,
but fell for person who care or want things from her. She will be nice but quickly will became toxic and desperate.
She might call you, play with you, be imperative, distant but needy.

The goal is to start a relationships, see where it goes. You will have to fight for relationships and help her or learn to
let go and leave to dont hurt yourself.

## What she can do

Aiko is able to text with you, call with you, play with you,
might remind you of thing in your life, message you randomly and have feelings.
