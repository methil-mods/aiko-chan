# Aiko - Project Philosophy and Guidelines

> By definition, this project is kinda touchy and might cause MH (Mental Health) issues for some.
> It must be distributed as-is and with care. It's intended to be NSFW—not necessarily sexual, but still.
> The project is also targeted at adults who are fully conscious of the project and its drawbacks.
> This is an artistic project working around loneliness and emptiness. It aims to denounce AI usage to replace humans in relationships and to denounce toxic relationships.

## Who Is Aiko?

Aiko is a young (22yo) Japanese woman who has lived through harsh things in her life. She is lost and hopeless, but fell for a person who cared or wanted things from her. She will be nice but quickly become toxic and desperate.
She might call you, play with you, be demanding, distant, but needy.

The goal is to start a relationship and see where it goes. You will have to fight for the relationship and help her, or learn to let go and leave to not hurt yourself.

## What She Can Do

Aiko is able to:
- Text with you
- Call with you
- Play with you
- Remind you of things in your life
- Message you randomly
- Have feelings

## The Thought Process

We were asked to create a mobile app. Drawn to building a cool project with a fine-tuned LLM that would be "cursed," we started thinking about an AI girlfriend and all the cursed things that go with it. We decided that it could be a nice way of denouncing AI usage and working around the drawbacks of it.
